What is Matrix?

Matrix is the new standard for open, decentralised communication. Freeing communication from proprietary silos, Matrix allows anyone to be free to pick who hosts their communication, without limiting their reach.

Learn more

Who are New Vector?

New Vector was founded in 2017 by the creators of Matrix, to grow the Matrix ecosystem and fund development of the protocol. The team has been building messaging & VoIP solutions together since 2003.
