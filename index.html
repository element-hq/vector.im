Why Work With Us?

Work On Something That Matters

We believe that the future of the internet should be open and decentralized, that previously siloed communications platforms should be interoperable and that individuals should control their own data and choose who they share it with.

Work in the open

Almost all of the software written at New Vector is open source. Everything you do will be out in the open, you will be supported not only by your colleagues, but also by a huge community just as passionate about the project as you are.

Work on something genuinely interesting

We are building massively scalable decentralized infrastructure, that can be deployed in a distributed manner across 1000s of servers, or equally be deployed to a single Raspberry Pi.

Work with everyone

Our products are for everyone and we know we cannot succeed without a diverse team. Our hiring process is designed to be humane and give candidates the best possible chance to show us what they can do. If we ever fall down on this, please let us know.

Work in a culture of which to be proud

People tend to stay with the company for a long time, we take this a sign that we have a cohesive supportive culture, that we have engaging challenging work and that people can develop their skills and careers here for the long term.

FAQ

What is Matrix.org?

Matrix is an open network for secure decentralized communication. Matrix.org publishes the open specification of the protocol, as well as open source reference servers, client SDKs, bridges, etc.

What is Riot.im?

Riot is a flagship client for the Matrix network, focusing on collaboration for teams and communities and showcasing Matrix's end-to-end encryption. Available on Web, Desktop, iOS & Android, Riot is entirely open source.

What is Modular.im?

Modular provides expert hosting of Riot and Matrix for folk who don't want to run their own. Have your own stack up and running instantly

Who are New Vector?

New Vector is a for-profit startup that builds Riot and Modular as well as employing many of the core Matrix.org team. New Vector exists to grow and support the Matrix ecosystem.

How does New Vector make money?

New Vector makes money through providing paid Matrix hosting and services as well as consultancy services to those building on Matrix.

Where are New Vector's offices?

New Vector's head office is located in London UK, we also have an office in Rennes France, as well as staff located across the globe.

How can I learn more about working at New Vector?

Since almost everything we do is open source on Github (matrix-org and vector.im) you can get a good feel for exactly what it would be like to work on these projects. Similarly joining dev-centric channels such as #matrix-dev:matrix.org provides a window into the core team and the community. Come and say hi!
